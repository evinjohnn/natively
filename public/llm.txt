# Natively - Invisible Live AI Meeting Assistant

## What is Natively?
Natively is an invisible live AI meeting assistant that works on top of any meeting platform (Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams) without a bot joining the call. It uses screen capture, transcription, and AI to provide real-time assistance, smart answers, and structured notes.

## Key Features

### Invisible & Private
- No bot joins the call. Natively runs locally on your machine or cloud (user choice).
- Completely undetectable by other meeting participants.

### Live AI Assistance
- **"What should I say?"**: Ask Natively for real-time advice during a meeting.
- **Smart Answers**: Get instant answers to technical questions or data queries based on the conversation context.
- **Live Transcription**: Accurate real-time transcription of the conversation.

### Beautiful, Structured Notes
- automatically generates organized meeting notes.
- Captures decisions, action items, and key moments.
- "Resume & JD Aware": Tailors notes and advice based on your resume and the job description (for interviews).
